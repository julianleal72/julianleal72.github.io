About Me

I'm a full-stack developer with experience in React.js, Ruby on Rails, Javascript/TypeScript, and Python, among others.
Always working to expand my technical talent stack.

I aspire to combine the solution driven aspects of coding with a sense of artistic drive and creativity, to create applications that are both useful and appealing to users. In doing so, I hope to work with like-minded people and through learning from and teaching each other form lasting bonds.

Graduate of Flatiron School's Software Engineering program, with a B.S. in Geology from the University of Maryland.

When I'm not programming, I enjoy anything involving music, reading sci-fi/fantasy novels or manga, hiking, kayaking, and playing trading card games.
